Kate Reinders

$1 million

Kate Reinders is an American actress-singer. Initially, she had her career beginnings in the 1990s. She first appeared in the 1995 film While You Were Sleeping. Later in the 2000s, she started her prolific stage career. In 2004, she played a small role in the movie Kinsey. She then had one-time roles in such television shows as Blind Justice and Jeopardy!

She next had a notable guest role as Ginger in Ugly Betty. She went on to play Paula in the sitcom Sherri. During this period, she had performances in the films Certainty, Grudge Match, and Such Good People. From 2012 to 2013, she starred as Kelly in Work It. As of 2019, she stars as Miss Jenn in High School Musical: The Musical: The Series.
