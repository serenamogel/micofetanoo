My Unorthodox Life season 2 star Nathalie Ulander is no longer with Miriam Haart

May 2024 · 3 minute read

As “My Unorthodox Life” season 2 unfolds, one of the new stars who has taken the spotlight is Nathalie Ulander. Introduced as Miriam Haart’s girlfriend, their love story began in July 2021, and fans are eager to learn more about Nathalie and the current status of their relationship.

Miriam, renowned as one of Julia Haart’s daughters, ventured into the world of dating apps and serendipitously found love in the form of Nathalie, a Swedish tennis player. Their relationship blossomed and became Instagram-official over a year ago.

So, who exactly is Nathalie Ulander, and are she and Miriam still together? Viewers of “My Unorthodox Life” season 2 have witnessed their deep connection, so let’s delve into their story and discover what has transpired since Netflix concluded filming for the series.

Meet Nathalie Ulander

Nathalie Ulander is a Swedish tennis player who became romantically involved with Miriam Haart. Throughout “My Unorthodox Life,” viewers learned that Nathalie faced the possibility of returning to Sweden due to her visa status. In an effort to secure her stay in the USA, the couple even considered the option of marriage to obtain a green card.

After her student visa expired, Nathalie took a job at Haartsphere, the new startup launched by Julia and Miriam Haart. Her primary objective is to obtain a work visa to avoid deportation, having initially moved to the USA to attend Tarleton State University in Texas.

Nathalie’s journey in the world of tennis began at Stockholm Idrottsgymnasium before she pursued her passion in Texas, where she played tennis from 2018 to 2020, as indicated in her player bio. Subsequently, she relocated from Texas to Manhattan, New York, and celebrated her graduation from Queens College with heartfelt pictures.

Nathalie Ulander’s Age

Nathalie is 23 years old, as revealed in her IFT Tennis bio. In comparison, Miriam was born on January 25, 2000, making her 22 years old at the time of writing. On Miriam’s twenty-second birthday, Nathalie extended warm wishes to her co-star.

Nathalie boasts an impressive ten-year career as a high-performance athlete that began during her teenage years, competing on an international level. She represented Queens College of New York on the NCAA DII Women’s Tennis team and even served as the captain of the QC Women’s Tennis Team. During her tenure at Queens College, she pursued a double major in honors international business and economics and finance and business, a testament to her academic prowess.

Are Nathalie and Miriam Still Together?

Regrettably, Nathalie and Miriam are no longer a couple. Nathalie herself referred to Miriam as her “ex-girlfriend” in a TikTok video. The last post featuring both of them dates back to January 2022, with Nathalie referring to Miriam as her “best friend.”

Miriam officially confirmed her relationship with Nathalie on July 28, 2021, through an Instagram post that read, “Secret’s out, and so am I ❤️.” Subsequently, they shared numerous photos together. Although there have been no public announcements from Miriam regarding their breakup, it is evident that they have parted ways.

Despite the change in their relationship status, Miriam’s sister, Batsheva Haart, continues to engage with Nathalie on her Instagram posts, including comments on her recent video discussing work visas.

As Nathalie Ulander continues her journey beyond “My Unorthodox Life” season 2, her story remains one that has captured the curiosity of fans and viewers alike.
