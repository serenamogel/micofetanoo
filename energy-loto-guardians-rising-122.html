Energy Loto (Japanese: エネくじ Ene Lottery) is an Item card. It was first released as part of the Guardians Rising expansion.

Card text

Guardians Rising print
Look at the top 7 cards of your deck. You may reveal an Energy card you find there and put it into your hand. Shuffle the other cards back into your deck.
You may play as many Item cards as you like during your turn (before your attack).
Astral Radiance print
Look at the top 7 cards of your deck. You may reveal an Energy card you find there and put it into your hand. Shuffle the other cards back into your deck.
You may play any number of Item cards during your turn.

Release information

This card was included in the Guardians Rising expansion with artwork by Yoshinobu Saito, first released in the Japanese Islands Await You expansion. It was reprinted in the Japanese GX Ultra Shiny subset. During the Sword & Shield Series, Energy Loto was reprinted in the Astral Radiance expansion with different artwork by ORBITALLINK Inc., first released in the Japanese Space Juggler expansion. In Japan, it was reprinted as a Mirror Holofoil S-P Promotional card and was available as a winner's prize at regional Champion's League events held during the 2022-2023 season. These included the 2023 Yokohama Champion's League, held at the Pacifico Yokohama on September 17 and 18, 2022[1] and the 2023 Kyoto Champion's League, held at the Kyoto Pulse Plaza on December 10 and 11, 2022.[2] At each event, the top 64 competitors from each League at the conclusion of the qualifying round received a copy of this print.[3] This print also features the Championship Series 2023 logo in the bottom left corner of the illustration.
