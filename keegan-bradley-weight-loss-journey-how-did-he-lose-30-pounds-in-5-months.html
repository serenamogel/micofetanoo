Keegan Bradley Weight Loss Journey: How Did He Lose 30 Pounds In 5 Months

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Fans of Keegan Bradley have been talking about his weight loss since the footballer dropped 30 pounds in only five months.

American professional golfer Keegan Bradley competes on the PGA Tour. His amateur career was fruitful, as he won the Northeast Amateur in 2008 as well as the New England Amateur Championship in 2007.

In 2008, he became a professional and joined the Nationwide Tour, where he won the 2010 Mexico Open.

Bradley won the Byron Nelson Championship in his debut tour appearance in 2011 to earn a spot on the PGA Tour. As a result, he became the first golfer in PGA Tour history to achieve success on his tour debut.

In 2012, he won the WGC-Bridgestone Invitational to cap off his debut season.

Among his noteworthy accomplishments are his 2011 PGA tournament triumph and his status as the only golfer to ever win a major tournament with a long putter. In 2012 and 2014, he was a member of the American Ryder Cup squad.

Keegan Bradley’s Story of Weight Loss: How He Dropped 30 Pounds in Just Five Months

Bradley’s remarkable weight reduction journey has also garnered media attention. Bradley shed thirty pounds in only five months—a noteworthy feat that called for commitment, perseverance, and a readiness to alter his way of life.

According to Bradley, he started the diet after the BMW Championship in August of last year and worked with UpWellness, a nutrition company, to lose weight.

Sugar-filled beverages like soda and Gatorade were also outlawed, according to Golf Digest, and Bradley’s workouts switched to almost exclusively cardio exercises like the elliptical or stationary cycle.

When he started the regimen, his weight was 220 pounds. He’s already lost about 190 pounds and is prepared to resume a diet low in carbohydrates.

Bradley agreed to change his diet to almost exclusively consist of meat.

Bradley said, “You can eat anything that walks, swims, or flies, and I did a lot of fruit as well.” His transformation has been so amazing that several colleagues have questioned if he is sick.

Eating only beef that has been grass-fed is the key. No vegetables are present. Furthermore, there aren’t any starches, sauces, sides, or even ketchup. All I use for seasoning is salt, pepper, and hot sauce.

By the time Bradley got to Japan for the Zozo Championship in mid-October, he had dropped back down to 190 pounds, but he felt like he was swinging more easily and hadn’t lost any distance or swing speed.

Following a T-5 finish at the Sanderson Farms Championship to begin the fall season, Bradley won by a stroke at Accordia Golf Narshino Country Club after playing four great rounds, including a final two-under 68.

Does Keegan Bradley Have a Spouse? Introducing Jillian Stacey, His Wife

Yes, in December 2016 at the Henry Morrison Flagler Museum in Palm Beach, Florida, he wed Jillian Stacey, his longtime partner.

The pair announced soon after getting married that they were expecting their first child in 2012. The couple began dating in 2012.

They welcomed their son Logan James Bradley into the world on November 13, 2017. When Bradley defeated Justin Rose in a playoff to win the 2018 BMW Championship—his last PGA Tour triumph—both of them were there to celebrate.

Jillian was born on October 29, 1987, making her the year before her husband, the major champion. They both grew up in Vermont, where Jillian and Keegan’s sister were childhood friends.

After completing her education at Hartford High School, she accompanied her elder sister, Meggan, to Eastern Connecticut State University.

Bradley’s professional golf career has had consistent support from Stacey, who regularly goes with him to events and activities. They make a cute pair and are often seen together on social media.
